ECDC to AROOM

Our firm was started as ECDC INTERIOR DESIGN DIVISION in the year 2011, and now we renamed it as AROOM INTERIORS in 2021. Every AROOM Design practice carries out basic and applied research in addition to project works, on issues of direct benefit to our clients. Our purpose is to create value that maximizes design's full potential for higher performance. Our company is enriched with experience of 10 years in interior and exterior design (Modular kitchen, Interior and Exterior design, Architectural design and many more).
